Study Tips

  • 🎧
    Listen every day

    Even 10 minutes of English podcasts or YouTube builds your ear for natural speech faster than any textbook.

  • ✍️
    Write, then review

    Write a short paragraph daily on any topic. Bring it to class — we will review it together.

  • 🔁
    Spaced repetition

    Review new vocabulary at increasing intervals (1 day → 3 days → 1 week). Use Quizlet or Anki to automate this.

  • 🗣️
    Speak out loud

    Read passages aloud, shadow native speakers or record yourself. Speaking fluency only improves by speaking.

學習技巧

  • 🔊
    從第一天開始練習聲調

    普通話有四個聲調，聲調不同意思迥異。每天跟讀錄音，讓耳朵建立正確語感。

  • ✍️
    動手寫漢字

    手寫漢字能加深記憶。每天練習5–10個新字，配合筆順動畫效果更佳。

  • 📺
    收看中文影視

    搭配中文字幕觀看中文劇或新聞，同時強化聽力和認字能力。

  • 🗣️
    與母語者對話

    使用 Tandem 或 HelloTalk 與母語者練習，真實對話是提升口語的最快方式。

🎬
